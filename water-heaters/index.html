Austin's hard water eats water heaters early.

Mineral scale from Austin's hard water builds up inside the tank, making it pop, rumble, run lukewarm, and fail years before its time. A flush can buy time; past a point, replacement is the honest answer.

Should I repair or replace my water heater?+

Generally, repair makes sense when the unit is under about 10 years old and only a few components are involved. Replacement is often smarter when it is over 10 years old, has needed multiple repairs, or efficiency has dropped. We give you an honest recommendation, not a sales pitch.
